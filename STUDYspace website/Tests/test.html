STUDYspace is a procrastination-prevention/productivity program which attempts to help students struggling to stay on track and tackle procrastination. We attempt to help you study distraction free by getting you to select the websites of your choice that pose the biggest threat to your productivity. We then block these websites until you choose to disable it or until a time which is also chosen by you runs out.
