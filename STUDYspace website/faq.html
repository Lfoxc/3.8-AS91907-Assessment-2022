FAQ

Welcome to our Frequently Asked Questions page! Here you can find the who, what, when, how and why regarding STUDYspace. If you are unable to find the answers you area looking for, please email us here and we will be sure to get back to you ASAP!

STUDYspace is a procrastination-prevention/productivity program which attempts to help students struggling to stay on track and tackle procrastination. We attempt to help you study distraction free by getting you to select the websites of your choice that pose the biggest threat to your productivity. We then block these websites until you choose to disable it or until a time which is also chosen by you runs out.
There are plenty of productivity sites out there, but STUDYspace is different. There are Pomodoro timers that do not attempt to reinforce your focused learning, and there are website blockers that have a strict schedule for the day and do not unblock websites or applications until the next day. STUDYspace tries to combine all of these concepts and allow flexibility for any situation you might face! Also, some productivity sites provide users with a free trial before they are required to make monthly payments if they want to keep using their product. Here at STUDYspace we think there should not be a price on progress!

Yes you can! If you join STUDYspace as a guest, you will still have the option to change to an account. It will follow the same process as if you had originally chosen the account option. Read the question above to find out more about account registration.
The STUDYspace app and browser extension serves the same purpose: chose a work mode and block websites of your choosing. However, the biggest differences between the two is accounts, save files and data analysis. Save files will save the mode you have used, time (if using Pomodoro mode) and the websites you have blocked. Accounts will ensure you have access to these save files on different devices or if you delete and reinstall the app. Our Data analysis looks at which websites you have blocked the most and which modes you have used the most, as well as how long you have been using STUDYspace.
STUDYspace will be available on mobile as an app, since our extension is a Google Chrome Browser Extension, which cannot be used on mobile devices. However, the app will be released after the extension.
