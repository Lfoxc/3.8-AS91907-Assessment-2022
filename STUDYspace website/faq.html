FAQ

Welcome to our Frequently Asked Questions page! Here you can find the who, what, when, how and why regarding STUDYspace. If you are unable to find the answers you area looking for, please email us here and we will be sure to get back to you ASAP!

STUDYspace is a procrastination-prevention/productivity program which attempts to help students struggling to stay on track and tackle procrastination. We attempt to help you study distraction free by getting you to select the websites of your choice that pose the biggest threat to your productivity. We then block these websites until you choose to disable it or until a time which is also chosen by you runs out.

About us

STUDYspace inc. is a New Zealand-based organization which looks to help students with their studies. Our previous project, DITCS, is a Digital Technologies help site created for Digital Technology students in NCEA.
