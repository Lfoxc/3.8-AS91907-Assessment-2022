Downloading STUDYspace

As of now, STUDYspace is still in development. This means you cannot download the extension or app at this time, but here is how we imagine the downloading process will look like + additional steps to setting up STUDYspace.

Chrome Browser Extension

Step one: Downloading the extension.

In order to download our Google Chrome Extension, there will be a link that will transport you to our official page in Google Chrome's web store. Once the page has loaded, click the 'Add to Chrome' button.

Step two: Setting up the extension.

Once you have added our extension to your Chrome page, it should show up when you click on the puzzle icon besides the search bar. The extension will display your available options for studying. Go to the 'Choosing your work mode' section to find out what to do next.

App

Step one: Downloading our app.

Once our app is ready for use, there will be a link at the top of the page to redirect you to Play Store. Or you can search it up on Play Store. Once you find our page just click on 'Install'.

Step two: Choosing an account or guest.

Open the app once it is installed, and choose how you would like to use STUDYspace. Either as a Guest or through an account. If you use a guest account your progress will not be saved if you delete the app, whereas if you use an account it will. If you already have an account you will be able to log in.

Step three I: Setting up your STUDYspace account.

If you choose the 'Account' option, you will be required to tell us your email address so that we can send you a registration code. You will receive an email containing the code, which you can then copy and paste into our app. The code we send you is case sensitive, so make sure to type in the same code as the email.

Step three II: Continuing your STUDYspace setup as a guest.

If you choose the 'Guest' option, instead of needing to register with your email address, you will be taken to the dashboard with the opportunity to follow a tutorial for how to use STUDYspace. Your save files will not be saved if you delete and reinstall our app, or if you install it on another device.

Choosing your work mode

Manual mode

In Manual mode, you will have the option to choose any website or app for us to block. Unlike our timed modes, you can deactivate and reactivate the block whenever you want.

Pomodoro mode

In Pomodoro mode, you can choose a time of up to 25 minutes and a rest break time of up to 15 minutes. Once you have chosen your times you will then move on to chosing the websites you want us to block.

Exam mode

Compared to the other modes of STUDYspace, Exam mode does not allow you to choose what websites or apps you want blocked. Instead, it will block everything but google urls and apps. You can also choose a time between 45 minutes and 3 hours.
